Welcome to the Course

This course offers an in-depth exploration of Assamese literature, tracing its historical evolution and examining key works and literary figures that have shaped this rich literary tradition.

About the Course

This course is designed for literature enthusiasts, students, researchers, and anyone interested in learning about Assamese literature. It covers various genres, themes, and narratives, providing a comprehensive understanding of Assamese literary works.

Target Audience

Suitable for beginners, students, and professionals who have an interest in Assamese literature.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify key historical periods and literary movements in Assamese literature.
  • Analyze the works of prominent Assamese writers and poets.
  • Understand the cultural and historical context of Assamese literary works.
  • Explore the different genres and themes prevalent in Assamese literature.
  • Engage critically with Assamese literary texts and appreciate their artistic value.

Course Modules

  1. Module 1: Introduction to Assamese Literature - Estimated Duration: 2 hours
  2. Module 2: Early Assamese Literature - Estimated Duration: 3 hours
  3. Module 3: Modern Assamese Literature - Estimated Duration: 4 hours
  4. Module 4: Contemporary Trends and Themes - Estimated Duration: 3 hours
  5. Module 5: Literary Analysis and Interpretation - Estimated Duration: 4 hours

Resources & Materials

This course includes a variety of learning materials:

  • Quizzes for self-assessment
  • Discussion prompts for reflection

Engagement & Assessment

We offer several ways to engage and assess learners:
